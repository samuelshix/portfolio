The Hilite Newspaper

I was once involved in our high school's award-winning school newspaper, HiLite. As managing editor my senior year, I supervised page editors, reporters, photographers and graphics artists, streamlining the newspaper production process; that year, our newspaper won a Pacemaker Award. Another component of this role was to mentor staff about elements of journalism every week in presentation format. Check out some of my newspaper designs on the home page.

CCCI English Camp

The summer after my sophomore year, I travelled to Taiwan in order to lead an English camp teaching young kids English. Through this experience I realized that I loved to teach and thoroughly enjoyed working with other members on my team to draft lesson plans, lead fun activities, and prepare other logistics like supplies and event scheduling. This was truly a rewarding experience.

Macy's Case Competition

Me and my team were finalists for the Macy's Case Competition, held among all freshman business students taking Honors Business Presentations. The premise of the competition is present a strategy for Macy's to make its smartphone app more appealing to Generation Z, as Macy's has low engagement with this upcoming generation.
